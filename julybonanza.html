Bird Bam Bonanza

Official Rules

1. Sponsor

Bird Bam Bonanza (“Promotion”) is sponsored by Bird Bam Mahjong Club, located in the Encino Town Center, 17200 Ventura Blvd, Ste 115, Encino, CA 91316 (“Sponsor”).

2. Promotion Period

The Promotion runs from Wednesday, July 1, 2026 through Thursday, July 30, 2026 (the “Promotion Period”). All entries must be received during the Promotion Period to be eligible for the drawing.

3. Eligibility

  • Open to individuals who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.
  • Open to legal residents of the United States who can lawfully participate in the Promotion.
  • Employees, owners, and operators of Bird Bam Mahjong Club, and members of their immediate household, are not eligible to win.
  • Void where prohibited by law.

By entering, participants agree to be bound by these Official Rules and Sponsor’s decisions, which are final and binding in all matters related to this Promotion.

4. PlaybyPoint Registration Requirement

  • All entrants must have or create a free Bird Bam Mahjong Club account in PlaybyPoint, using this link: https://birdbammahjongclub.playbypoint.com/users/sign_up .
  • The name, email, and phone number written on the entry slip must match the entrant’s PlaybyPoint account information.

Entries that do not match an existing or newly created free PlaybyPoint account may be deemed invalid at Sponsor’s sole discretion.

5. How to Enter

A. In-club Entry (Play-based Entries)

During the Promotion Period:

1. Open play attendance entry

  • Each day an eligible entrant checks in and pays for an open play session at Bird Bam Mahjong Club in July, they will receive one (1) free entry slip.
  • The entrant must clearly print their full name, email address, and phone number on the slip and drop it into the Bird Bam Bonanza entry box on the same day.

2. Mahjong bonus entries

  • Each time an eligible entrant gets mahjong during that day’s open play session (according to the club’s standard rules of play), they will receive one (1) bonus entry slip for that mahjong.
  • The entrant must clearly print their name, email, and phone on each bonus slip and drop it into the Bird Bam Bonanza entry box before leaving that day.

There is no separate charge or fee to receive entry slips; they are a promotional bonus associated with participation in open play during the Promotion Period.

B. Free Mail-in Entry (Alternate Method of Entry — AMOE)

No purchase or gameplay is required to enter via the mail-in method.

To enter without attending open play:

  • During the Promotion Period, send a stamped, self-addressed envelope (SASE) to:
    Bird Bam Mahjong Club – Bird Bam Bonanza
    17200 Ventura Blvd
    Ste 115
    Encino, CA 91316
  • Sponsor will mail back one (1) entry slip to the address on the SASE.
  • The entrant must clearly print their full name, email address, and phone number on the slip, ensuring it matches a free PlaybyPoint account as described in Section 4.
  • The completed entry slip must be returned by mail to the same address above and received by Sponsor no later than 4:00 PM Pacific Time on Thursday, July 30, 2026.
  • Limit one (1) mail-in entry per person for the entire Promotion Period.
  • Mail-in entries will be treated the same as in-club entries in the drawing.

Sponsor is not responsible for lost, late, misdirected, damaged, illegible, incomplete, or postage-due mail-in requests or entries.

6. Entry Conditions

  • All required fields on the entry slip must be complete and legible.
  • Duplicate, mechanically reproduced, or automated entries are not permitted.
  • Incomplete, illegible, or mismatched entries may be disqualified at Sponsor’s sole discretion.
  • By submitting an entry, entrant confirms that all information provided is accurate and that they meet eligibility requirements.

7. Prizes

There are ten (10) prizes available in Bird Bam Bonanza:

1. Grand Prize

  • Unlimited free open play at Bird Bam Mahjong Club for the month of August 2026.
  • Valid only for standard open play sessions; does not include private bookings, tournaments, special events, private events, paid classes, or any session specifically excluded by Sponsor.
  • Advance reservations may be required, and play is subject to table availability and club capacity.

2. Other Prizes (nine total)

  • 3 free open play passes (one prize; valid for three standard open play sessions).
  • 1 free open play pass.
  • 1 free open play pass.
  • $50 gift certificate to spend in the Bird Bam Mahjong Club shop.
  • $25 gift certificate to spend in the Bird Bam Mahjong Club shop.
  • Bird Bam Mahjong Club play mat.
  • Pair of engraved metal mahjong dice.
  • Mahjong accessory bag.
  • Mahjong tile necklace.

Approximate retail values (ARVs) may be provided by Sponsor upon request. Prizes are non-transferable and may not be substituted, except that Sponsor reserves the right to substitute a prize of equal or greater value in case of unavailability.

8. Prize Drawing and Odds

  • All valid entries received during the Promotion Period (in-club and mail-in) will be combined in one pool.
  • Prize winners will be selected in a random drawing conducted by Sponsor at the end of open play on Thursday, July 30, 2026 at Bird Bam Mahjong Club, or at a reasonably comparable time if circumstances require adjustment.
  • Odds of winning depend on the total number of eligible entries received. Each valid entry slip has an equal chance of being drawn.
  • Limit one prize per person/PlaybyPoint account unless Sponsor chooses, at its sole discretion, to award multiple prizes to the same person.

Sponsor’s decisions regarding winner selection and prize awards are final and binding.

9. Winner Notification and Prize Claim

  • Winners will be notified using the email and/or phone number associated with their PlaybyPoint account and entry slip.
  • Winners may be required to show identification or verify their PlaybyPoint account and entry details.
  • If a winner cannot be contacted within 7 days after initial notification, fails to respond, is found ineligible, or declines the prize, Sponsor may select an alternate winner from remaining eligible entries.
  • Some prizes may need to be picked up in person at the club within a reasonable time frame (for example, within 30 days of notification). Failure to pick up may result in forfeiture.

Any applicable taxes, fees, or reporting obligations related to prizes are the responsibility of the winners.
